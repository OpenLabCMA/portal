Github Repo Wiki Inventory

The CMA OpenLab is a collaborative initiative focused on creative experimentation and open research. We are an inclusive, low-friction creative environment for CMA students and faculty to learn, create and explore. Think of it as a classroom, makerspace, art studio, tinkering workshop, and social hub all under one roof open 24/7. To get started read our code of conduct and check our wiki page for the introduction of the lab's facilities. We maintain a Github Repository with useful code schematics etc. You can browse the current inventory of lab equipment and materials.

OpenLab Access Agreement Location: CMA Lab E2 L3 East End Hours: Open 24/7 Maintenance and Clean-Up Always allow time to leave the space and equipment in the same condition as you found it. Dispose of trash promptly. Store projects in your designated area. Safety Always handle equipment with care and follow the operating instructions. Report any safety hazards or concerns to your course instructors immediately. Do not leave machines running unattended. Use Notes Fabric scissors are never to be used with other materials. Pin art into walls using only the thin pins that we show you, and hang less than 300 g. It is ok to play the piano. Reporting Damaged Equipment If any equipment or supplies are found to be malfunctioning or broken, send a WeChat or email to the Contacts immediately (or use the red button on the OpenLab website). Don't be shy, it's ok even if you broke it, just let us know asap.
