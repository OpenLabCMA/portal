The CMA OpenLab is a collaborative initiative focused on creative experimentation and open research. We are an inclusive, low-friction creative environment for CMA students and faculty to learn, create and explore. Think of it as a classroom, makerspace, art studio, tinkering workshop, and social hub all under one roof open 24/7. To get started read our code of conduct and check our wiki page for the introduction of the lab's facilities. We maintain a Github Repository with useful code schematics etc. You can browse the current inventory of lab equipment and materials.

OpenLab Access Agreement Location: CMA Lab E2 L3 East End Hours: Open 24/7 Maintenance and Clean-Up Always leave the space and equipment in the same condition as you found it. Return tools and materials to their designated storage areas after use. Turn off all the powered equipment when you are done. Dispose of trash promptly. Store your projects in the designated area (grab a container and label it). Safety Always handle equipment with care and follow the operating instructions. Use the wiki to familiarize yourself with the equipment before using it. Report any safety hazards or concerns to your course instructors or the lab manager immediately. Do not leave machines running unattended. Use Notes Fabric scissors are never to be used with other materials. Pin your projects to the walls using only the thin pins that we show you, and hang less than 300 g. It is ok to play the piano. Reporting Damaged Equipment If any equipment or supplies are found to be malfunctioning or broken, send a WeChat or email to the Contacts immediately (or use the red button on the OpenLab website to open an issue). Don't be shy, it's ok even if you broke it, just let us know asap.
